### About the host — Moss (YC F25)
Moss is a real-time semantic-search runtime for conversational AI / AI agents. It runs search locally inside the agent runtime for **sub-10ms lookups with zero infrastructure**, built in **Rust and WebAssembly** — avoiding the 100–500ms latency of remote database calls during multi-turn conversations. It powers voice agents, copilots, and multimodal apps. Built by InferEdge Inc. (San Francisco, YC-backed); lists Microsoft, EPAM, UC Berkeley, Carnegie Mellon, Podium, and Stanford among users. ([moss.dev](https://www.moss.dev/), [Moss on YC](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/moss), [Launch YC: Moss](https://www.ycombinator.com/launches/Oiq-moss-real-time-semantic-search-for-conversational-ai))

## 2. Emotionally Intelligent AI Hackathon with ElevenLabs (AI Tinkerers, NYC)

Part of the **ElevenLabs Worldwide Hackathon** running across ~30 cities; the NYC edition is hosted by **AI Tinkerers** and themed around the "emotional layer" for voice AI.

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Emotionally Intelligent AI Hackathon with ElevenLabs |
| **Host** | AI Tinkerers — New York City chapter |
| **Sponsor** | ElevenLabs (+ partners) |
| **Date** | June 6, 2026 (in-person only — no remote entries) |
| **Format** | Evening hackathon; teams of up to 4 (solo builders can form teams on site) |
| **Scope** | Worldwide event across ~30 cities exploring conversational agents |

**What to build:** voice-to-voice or multimodal agents that listen, reason, and respond; turning browsers, voices, and APIs into agents that act on intent and respond in real time; experiments that push conversation beyond text — ambient, embodied, or emotional interfaces.

**Prizes:**
- **1st place (all locations):** Apple AirPods + $6k in Stripe credits
- **2nd & 3rd:** local prizes announced at each venue
- Local winners advance to a **global finalist pool**, judged by an international panel including the ElevenLabs founders and partners

**Submission:** a short demo video (≤ 2 minutes) is strongly encouraged.

**Typical schedule** (AI Tinkerers evening format — confirm on the event page): doors ~6:00pm, team formation 6:00–6:15pm, kickoff talk 6:15pm, building 6:30–9:30pm, finalist presentations, wrap-up by ~10:30pm.
